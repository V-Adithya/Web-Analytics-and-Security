About Us


Established in 2013, Presidency University offers a vibrant learning experience within its sprawling 70-acre campus. Recognized by UGC and accredited with an "A+" grade by NAAC, we empower students across eight diverse schools: Engineering, Management, Design, Information Science, Media Studies, Commerce, Economics, and Law. Explore 70+ programs at undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral levels, designed to prepare you for success in today's dynamic world. Our focus on experiential learning, international collaborations, and industry-aligned curriculum will equip you with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to thrive.

Events

A burst of color takes over Presidency University, Bangalore, on Ethnic Day. Students don their traditional attire, showcasing India's diverse cultural tapestry. From vibrant sarees to regional headwear, the campus transforms into a visual feast. The day comes alive with traditional dance performances, delectable food stalls, and displays of art and craft, each representing different ethnicities. This celebration fosters understanding and appreciation for the university's rich cultural mosaic.
